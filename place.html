About Nigeria

Nigeria, often called the "Giant of Africa," is a vibrant and diverse country located in West Africa. It is home to over 200 million people and boasts more than 250 ethnic groups, with rich cultural traditions, music, and languages. Nigeria is known for its dynamic cities like Lagos and Abuja, beautiful landscapes such as the Zuma Rock and Obudu Cattle Ranch, and natural wonders like the Niger River and Aso Rock. Nigerian cuisine is bold and flavorful, featuring dishes like jollof rice, pounded yam, egusi soup, and suya. The country is also a powerhouse of creativity, especially in literature, fashion, and its globally recognized film industry, Nollywood.
